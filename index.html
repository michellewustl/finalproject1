Clicking Bach: a Tool Demonstration

Instructions

This is an experimental demonstration of a web-based tool for more easily navigating music scores. The page contains a music sheet and a recording of the same piece. Click the play button to start playing the recording, then use your keyboard or the buttons to sync up timestamps in the recording to their places on the score. You can fine-tune any timestamps just by clicking them and manually entering your desired value, or adjusting them with the arrows. Once you are finished, you can click the "View & Play From Score" button, where now the long musical score will scroll along with the recording as you listen. Enjoy the ride!
